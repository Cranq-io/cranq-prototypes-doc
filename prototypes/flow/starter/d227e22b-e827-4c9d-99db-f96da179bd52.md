# Starter

_[flow/Starter]_

![icon](</assets/icons/d6c7d254-d4ea-4185-a98a-29b8942d39cc.png>)

Sends the signal null@"start" asynchronously right after the node was created at runtime.<br>

Mostly used to kick-start the entire program.<br>
